Sheet Pan Chicken with Mozzarella, Pesto, and Broccoli

Easy one-dish chicken dinner baked on a sheet pan with broccoli and tomatoes.

Ingredients

    2 pounds boneless chicken breasts
    2 teaspoons garlic salt
    1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
    6 tablespoons pesto
    2 Roma (plum) tomatoes, thinly sliced
    1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    1 head broccoli, cut into florets
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    salt to taste

Steps!!

  1. Step 1 Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Lightly grease a large rimmed baking sheet.
  2. Step 2 Place chicken on the prepared baking sheet and sprinkle with garlic salt and black pepper. Spread pesto over chicken and top with tomatoes and cheese.
  3. Step 3 Toss broccoli and oil together in a bowl. Place broccoli around chicken on the baking sheet. Sprinkle salt and pepper on top.
  4. Step 4 Bake in the preheated oven until broccoli is tender and chicken is no longer pink in the center, 15 to 20 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
